About

FurFind is a web-based stray animals adoption system that connects rescued cats and dogs with potential adopters. It allows users to browse available animals, view their details, and submit adoption applications easily and securely.

Mission

FurFind is committed to improving the stray animal adoption process by providing a smart and user-friendly platform that connects animal shelters, rescue organizations, and responsible adopters. The system aims to organize animal data, streamline adoption procedures, and match pets with suitable owners based on their needs and adopter profiles. Through technology and compassion, FurFind strives to reduce stray animal overpopulation and promote responsible, humane pet adoption.

Vision

To build a compassionate and well-organized community where every stray animal is given the opportunity to be seen, cared for, and placed in a loving forever home through an accessible and efficient adoption system.
